Welcome to PATHOS-II

Deep beneath the Atlantic, the last remnants of humanity survive within a decaying research facility. A world of crushing pressure, absolute darkness, and creeping dread. But also, a world of breathtaking, desolate beauty.

The Coin Toss

At its core, SOMA is a terrifying exploration of identity. It poses a simple question with a horrifying answer: If your consciousness is perfectly copied, which one is the real 'you'? The one who continues the journey, or the one left behind in the dark?
